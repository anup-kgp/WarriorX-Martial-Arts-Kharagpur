Our Mission

At WarriorX Martial Arts in Kharagpur, our mission is to empower individuals of all ages through expert martial arts training. We are committed to developing not just physical strength, but also mental discipline, self-confidence, and respect for oneself and others.

We believe that martial arts training transforms lives by teaching students to overcome challenges, set goals, and achieve excellence both on and off the mat.

Our Vision

To be the leading martial arts academy in Kharagpur, recognized for producing confident, disciplined, and skilled practitioners who excel in all areas of life.

We envision a community where every student, regardless of age or background, can discover their inner warrior and unlock their full potential through the practice of martial arts.

Our Legacy

WarriorX Martial Arts traces its roots back to 1995, when it was founded as Young Star Karate Center by renowned Sensei Srinivas Rao.

Sensei Srinivas Rao began teaching the art of self-defence through Shotokan Karate, along with Tiger Karate and intense full-contact combat training. He is widely respected for his exceptional fighting strategies and unique locking techniques.
